## 1. Executive Summary

**Immersive Family Dentistry** represents the next evolution of Lake Jeanette Family & Implant Dentistry — a technology-forward dental practice that fuses cutting-edge digital twin technology, FDA-cleared AI diagnostics, spatial computing, and in-house 3D manufacturing into a seamless, immersive patient experience.

### Vision
To transform the traditional, reactive dental visit into a proactive, interactive, and fully transparent healthcare experience by creating a dynamic "living" digital replica of every patient's oral anatomy.

### Mission
To leverage spatial computing, artificial intelligence, and advanced 3D manufacturing to make complex prosthodontic therapies and preventative family care more predictable, efficient, and accessible — empowering patients to actively participate in their own oral health journey.

### Value Proposition
**"Precision Care, Visualized."** — Unlike any dental practice in the Triad region, Immersive Family Dentistry allows patients to see their own anatomy in 3D, watch AI-generated diagnostics in real time, co-design their treatment plan, and often receive restorations the same day. This is dentistry you can see, understand, and trust.

### Key Differentiators
- **Dental Digital Twin Technology** — A dynamic, evolving 3D model of each patient's mouth
- **AI-Powered Diagnostics** — FDA-cleared platforms (Overjet, Pearl AI) that detect issues invisible to the naked eye
- **Same-Day Manufacturing** — In-house 3D printing and milling for rapid restorations
- **Immersive Visualization** — AR/XR interfaces let patients "step inside" their own mouth
- **World-Class Expertise** — Dr. Brenes is one of the most credentialed digital dentistry leaders in the world

## 3. Market Analysis

### Market Overview
The U.S. dental services market is valued at approximately $160 billion annually. The digital dentistry segment is one of the fastest-growing subsectors, driven by:
- Increasing patient demand for transparency and comfort
- Declining hardware costs for 3D scanners, CBCT, and printers
- FDA clearance of AI diagnostic platforms
- Millennial and Gen Z expectations for tech-integrated healthcare

### Target Market Segments

#### Primary: High-Value Restorative Patients
- Individuals needing complex implants, full-mouth rehabilitations, or cosmetic transformations
- Willingness to pay premium prices for specialist-level care
- Geographic reach: Entire Triad region (Greensboro, Winston-Salem, High Point) and beyond

#### Secondary: Tech-Savvy Families & Millennials
- Demographics that expect modern, efficient, transparent healthcare
- Strong social media presence for word-of-mouth marketing
- Value digital convenience (online booking, digital intake, remote monitoring)

#### Tertiary: Anxious Patients
- Patients who fear traditional dentistry
- Digital twin visualization reduces anxiety by demystifying treatments
- Seeing outcomes before treatment builds confidence and trust

### Local Demographics
- **Greensboro MSA Population:** ~775,000
- **Median Household Income (Lake Jeanette/North Elm area):** $85,000+
- **Key employers nearby:** HPU, UNCG, Moses Cone Health System, Cone Health
- **Growth corridor:** North Elm Street is one of Greensboro's most affluent commercial corridors

### Competitive Analysis

| Competitor Type | Typical Offering | Immersive Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| General Dentists | Basic digital X-rays, some have scanners | Full AI+digital twin stack; prosthodontist expertise |
| Other Prosthodontists | Specialist care but traditional workflows | Integrated digital platform; same-day manufacturing |
| Corporate/DSO Practices | High volume, lower cost | Boutique experience; world-class technology; 1-on-1 time |
| Cosmetic "Smile Centers" | Marketing-heavy, often general dentists | Board-certified prosthodontist; evidence-based digital workflow |

**No practice in the Greensboro/Triad region currently offers an integrated dental digital twin experience.** This is a first-mover advantage.
