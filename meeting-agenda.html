Business Formation & IP

Critical legal and business infrastructure — needed before beta launch

Step 1 — Form Paloma Health Technologies LLC

File LLC in North Carolina. Operating agreement reflecting 55/45 split (Christian/Chris). Registered agent, EIN, business bank account. Target: July 2026.

Step 2 — Trademark Applications

PALOMA™ — AI dental assistant brand name (Class 42: SaaS; Class 44: Healthcare).
MouthMap™ — 3D oral visualization technology (Class 9: Software; Class 42: SaaS).
USPTO filing ~$250–350 per class per mark. Target: July–August 2026.

Step 3 — Provisional Patent Application

AI-powered dental assistant with real-time 3D oral mapping, bilingual patient interaction, and clinical decision support. Provisional patent: $1,500–3,000. 12 months to file full patent.

Step 4 — HIPAA Compliance Framework

Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with AWS/Firebase. HIPAA-compliant file storage (S3). Privacy policy, consent forms, data handling procedures. Required before any PHI.

Step 5 — Domain & Brand Assets

Acquire production domain (palomahealth.com or similar). SSL certificate, professional email (team@palomahealth.com). Brand guidelines document.
