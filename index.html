Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Christian Brenes

DDS, MS — Prosthodontist

Originally from Costa Rica, Dr. Brenes is a dedicated prosthodontist specializing in esthetics and advanced dental implant solutions. As the Founding Director of Digital Dentistry at High Point University's Workman School of Dental Medicine, he bridges the gap between cutting-edge research and everyday patient care.

His philosophy is simple: every treatment should be meticulously planned and designed — not to achieve a "perfect smile," but rather a natural, custom smile that is uniquely yours.

Your Journey

The Lake Jeanette Patient Experience

We've reimagined every step of the dental visit to be transparent, comfortable, and empowering.

1

Digital Intake & Welcome

Complete forms digitally. Your medical history and lifestyle data feed into your personal digital twin, providing context for long-term oral health predictions.

2

AI-Assisted Scan & Cleaning

Our hygienists wear smart glasses that continuously record and analyze your session. While they clean and examine, AI is mapping your oral landscape in real time — flagging areas of concern, measuring pocket depths, and building a comprehensive diagnostic profile.

3

3D Digital Twin Creation

We capture high-resolution 3D scans and CBCT data. Combined with the AI insights from your cleaning session, your complete digital twin is assembled — a living 3D replica of your mouth with color-coded diagnostics overlaid.

4

AI-Powered Doctor Review

Before you sit with Dr. Brenes, our AI has already compiled its findings — highlighting concerns, recommending treatment options, and generating visual simulations. When Dr. Brenes reviews your case, he's armed with machine-level analysis combined with his clinical expertise.

5

Co-Design Your Treatment

Dr. Brenes walks you through the AI-generated insights on your digital twin. Together, you explore "what-if" treatment simulations, approve the design in 3D, and begin — often the same day.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

A prosthodontist is a dental specialist who has completed an additional 3 years of advanced training beyond dental school, focusing on the restoration and replacement of teeth. Dr. Brenes is one of the most trained dental specialists you can visit — with expertise in dental implants, full-mouth rehabilitation, veneers, and complex cosmetic cases.

A dental digital twin is a dynamic, evolving virtual replica of your mouth's anatomy — including teeth, gums, bone, and jaw structure. Unlike a simple 3D scan, your digital twin integrates data from multiple sources (intraoral scans, CBCT, AI diagnostics) and can simulate how treatments will look and function before we begin. It's like having a flight simulator for your dental care.

Yes! We accept all insurance plans that allow out-of-network providers, including PPO plans. We also offer flexible financing through CareCredit and Sunbit, as well as in-house membership plans for patients without insurance. Our goal is to make advanced dental care accessible to everyone.

Your first visit is a thorough, unhurried experience lasting about one hour. We'll capture 3D digital scans and X-rays of your teeth and jaw, perform a comprehensive exam, and create a customized treatment plan. You'll see your own oral anatomy in 3D and understand exactly where you stand and what options are available.

We offer multiple payment options to make implants accessible: CareCredit and Sunbit financing with flexible monthly plans, in-house membership discounts, and we work with your insurance to maximize your PPO out-of-network benefits. During your consultation, we'll walk through all available options.
