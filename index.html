Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Christian Brenes

DDS, MS — Prosthodontist

Originally from Costa Rica, Dr. Brenes is a dedicated prosthodontist specializing in esthetics and advanced dental implant solutions. With over 9 years as a university faculty member at renowned institutions, he bridges the gap between cutting-edge research and everyday patient care.

His philosophy is simple: every treatment should be meticulously planned and designed — not to achieve a "perfect smile," but rather a natural, custom smile that is uniquely yours.

  • Originally from Costa Rica — bilingual in English & Spanish
  • Founding Director of Digital Dentistry, HPU Workman School of Dental Medicine
  • Former Director, Digital Dentistry Master's Program, MUSC
  • Key Opinion Leader for Medit, SprintRay, Exocad, BlueSkyBio
  • Co-creator of the open-source Brenes-Farley Tooth Library

  • International speaker on digital workflows and implant dentistry

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

A prosthodontist is a dental specialist who has completed an additional 3 years of advanced training beyond dental school, focusing on the restoration and replacement of teeth. Dr. Brenes is one of the most trained dental specialists you can visit — with expertise in dental implants, full-mouth rehabilitation, veneers, and complex cosmetic cases.

A dental digital twin is a dynamic, evolving virtual replica of your mouth's anatomy — including teeth, gums, bone, and jaw structure. Unlike a simple 3D scan, your digital twin integrates data from multiple sources (intraoral scans, CBCT, AI diagnostics) and can simulate how treatments will look and function before we begin. It's like having a flight simulator for your dental care.

We accept all dental insurance plans that allow out-of-network providers. We also offer flexible financing through CareCredit, Sunbit, and in-house payment plans to make advanced dental care accessible to everyone.

Your first visit is a thorough, unhurried experience lasting about one hour. We'll capture 3D digital scans and X-rays of your teeth and jaw, perform a comprehensive exam, and create a customized treatment plan. You'll see your own oral anatomy in 3D and understand exactly where you stand and what options are available.

We offer multiple payment options to make implants accessible: CareCredit and Sunbit financing with flexible monthly plans, in-house membership discounts, and we work with your insurance to maximize your PPO out-of-network benefits. During your consultation, we'll walk through all available options.
